Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm thrilled to share my journey as a draftsman with you.

As a draftsman with a passion for precision and creativity, I bring a wealth of experience to the table. Proficient in architectural, structural, and MEP drawings, I've been actively contributing to large-scale projects since 2018. My expertise extends beyond drafting, encompassing project management, space planning, and rough estimates. Equipped with a National Diploma in Construction Technology and certifications in BIM with Revit, I am adept at utilizing industry-standard software like AutoCAD, SDS2, SketchUp, and Lumion. With a strong foundation in mathematics and a knack for problem-solving, I strive for excellence in every aspect of my work. Fluent in Sinhala and English, with a working knowledge of Tamil, I am committed to effective communication and collaboration across diverse teams. Let's turn visions into reality, one drawing at a time.
